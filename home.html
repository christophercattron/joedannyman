Who's Joe?

I am an engineer, designer, forger, and halo fanatic. I was born and raised in Festus, Missouri, and attended Missouri University of Science and Technology in Rolla, Missouri where I studied Engineering Management. I’ve been heavily involved in the Halo community from the early days of Halo 3 to Halo 5 and Halo MCC on PC. I can’t wait to see what new creative opportunities come with the release of the new-and-improved Forge mode for Halo Infinite. I got the opportunity to play in the 2019 UGC Halo Classic Championship in St. Louis where I competed in the Halo 3 4v4 Slayer bracket on Joe's Beard Energy and pooped in a stall next to FlamesworD. I love making forge maps, getting a full Halo customs lobby, listening to records on vinyl, jamming on my electric guitar, and vibing with my homies in Rolla.
